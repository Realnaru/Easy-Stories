How it works

We use TPR Storytelling approach. TPR Storytelling (Teaching Proficiency through Reading and Storytelling or TPRS) is a method of teaching foreign languages. You can lear more here.

This website is for additional practice during studying one of following languages:

  • english
  • russian
  • corean
  • japanese
  • chinese

You need to:

  • Read a story
  • Listen to the audio
  • Read question and answers
  • Listen to the questions and try to answer out loud
  • Try to repeat the story by yourself

Repeat every story for one several days(up to one week), at least once a day, until you really get familiar with all words and expressions, then it's time to start reading a new story.
