Murzik Petrovich

Story

Once upon time there lived a cat. His name was Murzik Petrovich. He was not an ordinary cat but a grandnephew of the godfather cat. He had a joyful, rich life by a compassionate old lady in one of Saint-Peterburg's yards. She adopted him when he was a kitten. She brought him up in the best traditions of Saint-Peterburg's intellectuals. That's why he never peed under a coach and never shrieked. Very rarely he sweared in cat language with stray cats from the Godfather cat's clan. And only not to be bullied as other usual house cats.
